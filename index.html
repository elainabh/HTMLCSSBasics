Welcome

We'll play with clouds today. Play with the angles. Isn't that fantastic? You can just push a little tree out of your brush like that. It's amazing what you can do with a little love in your heart. We don't have to be committed. We are just playing here.

When you buy that first tube of paint it gives you an artist license. Life is too short to be alone, too precious. Share it with a friend. Absolutely no pressure. You are just a whisper floating across a mountain. We spend so much of our life looking - but never seeing. Now, we're going to fluff this cloud. The more we do this - the more it will do good things to our heart.
